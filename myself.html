About Me

Hello! I am Gor Khushbu, a dedicated Digital Literacy Trainer with Sai's Angel Foundation in Bidada, Kutch. My mission is to empower individuals with the skills they need to navigate the digital world confidently. Through various training programs and workshops, I strive to bridge the digital divide and promote technological literacy in our community.

At Sai's Angel Foundation, we are committed to making technology accessible and understandable for everyone. Our programs are designed to cater to all age groups and skill levels, ensuring that each participant receives personalized attention and support.
